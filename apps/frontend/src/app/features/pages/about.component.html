Our Mission

SouqSyria is dedicated to connecting the world with authentic Syrian craftsmanship, artisan products, and cultural heritage. We bridge the gap between master craftsmen in Syria and customers worldwide who appreciate quality, authenticity, and cultural significance.

سوق سوريا ملتزم بربط العالم بالحرفية السورية الأصيلة والمنتجات الحرفية والتراث الثقافي. نحن نسد الفجوة بين الحرفيين الماهرين في سوريا والعملاء في جميع أنحاء العالم الذين يقدرون الجودة والأصالة والأهمية الثقافية.

Heritage & Tradition

For millennia, Syria has been home to master artisans who have perfected crafts passed down through generations. From the legendary Damascus steel blades to the ancient tradition of Aleppo soap making, Syrian craftsmanship represents excellence in heritage arts.

handshake

Connecting Communities

SouqSyria was founded to preserve and promote Syrian cultural heritage through e-commerce. We work directly with artisans, cooperatives, and family workshops across Syrian governorates including Damascus, Aleppo, Homs, and Latakia to bring authentic products to global markets.

verified

Authenticity Guaranteed

Every product on SouqSyria undergoes rigorous authentication. We verify artisan credentials, inspect traditional methods, and ensure cultural accuracy. Our UNESCO Heritage certification process guarantees you receive genuine Syrian craftsmanship, not mass-produced imitations.
